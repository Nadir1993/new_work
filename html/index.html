Azerbaijan

Snoozing amid green pillows of beautifully forested mountains, Şəki (Sheki) is Azerbaijan’s loveliest town, dappled with tiled-roof old houses and topped off with a glittering little khan’s palace. In 2019 its old core was declared a Unesco World Heritage Site.

Norway

Anyone who arrives in Norway from a country with a high level of transmission (categorised as red) must provide a negative corona test upon arrival and, in addition, take a mandatory COVID-19 test at the border.
